Aboutus


Our mission is to inspire & unlock creativity with liberating technology.



A little background

2006

Project Rhino launches within creative agency FMG with a plan to create a platform that empowers companies to create rich, interactive, magazine-like content on the web.

2007

Project Rhino formally becomes Ceros (Rhino-ceros) and launches V1 of the platform.

2008

Dennis Publishing chooses Ceros as its exclusive partner to launch an interactive online-only magazine. Before the iPad or iPhone, Ceros and Dennis amass well over 100,000 weekly subscribers.

2012

Ceros is acquired by Crowd Fusion in the US. As part of the deal, current CEO and co-founder Simon Berg becomes chief executive of the newly restructured company with Greycroft investing $2.5M to kick start the mission.

2014

Now with 60 clients, including Elizabeth Arden, Virgin Atlantic, and Peugeot, Ceros raises $6.4 million in new funding from Sigma Prime, StarVest Partners, and Greycroft to execute on its mission.

2016

Ceros moves into its first New York HQ, complete with a British pub, themed conference rooms, and a secret breakout room behind a bookcase.

2017

Ceros celebrates its one-hundredth employee!

2019

Ceros expands its New York HQ, with over 200 employees in the office. MarkUp is born, and a small team is put together to get design and development off the ground.

2020

Ceros receives funding from Sumeru and transitions to a fully remote business in the wake of the pandemic. The Power of Creativity launches featuring product updates, presentations, and interviews with creative leaders around the world.

2021

MarkUp receives Product of the Day, Week, Month and Year on Product Hunt. Ceros hires its three-hundredth employee!

2022

Ceros goes through its biggest transformation yet, expanding into a suite of tools and services.
